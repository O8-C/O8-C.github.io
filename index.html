Introduction to Lasagna

Lasagna is one of the most beloved dishes in Italian cuisine, known for its layers of rich flavor and comforting textures. It's a dish that combines hearty ingredients like pasta, meat, cheese, and tomato sauce, creating a filling and satisfying meal perfect for any occasion.

The beauty of lasagna lies in its layers. Each component is stacked in a specific order to create a harmonious blend of textures and flavors. Whether you enjoy the traditional meat-based version or prefer a vegetarian twist, lasagna remains one of the most versatile and beloved meals in both Italian and global cuisines.
